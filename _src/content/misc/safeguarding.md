# Safeguarding

Safeguarding is our top priority at the Northwest Vipers. We are committed to providing a safe and positive environment for all our members, whether they are playing, coaching, or spectating.

## Our Commitment

The Northwest Vipers are committed to the safeguarding and protection of all children and vulnerable adults involved in our club. We believe that everyone has the right to participate in sport in a safe and enjoyable environment.

## Contact Information

If you have any concerns about the safety or well-being of any member of our club, please contact our Safeguarding Officer:

## Reporting Concerns

If you have any concerns about the safety or well-being of any member of our club, please follow the procedures outlined in our safeguarding policy. You can also contact the following organisations for support:

- **NSPCC:** [0808 800 5000](tel:08088005000)
- **Childline:** [0800 1111](tel:08001111)
- **NSPCC Northern Ireland:** [0808 800 5000](tel:08088005000)
- **NSPCC Republic of Ireland:** [0818 50 40 50](tel:0818504050)

## Training and Development

All our coaches and volunteers are required to undergo regular safeguarding training to ensure they are equipped to provide a safe environment for our members. We also provide safeguarding training for our players to help them understand their rights and responsibilities.
