### Match Report
# Dublin Rhinos vs. Galway Gryphons

- **Date:** March 8, 2026
- **Venue:** Westmanstown Sports Centre, Dublin
- **Competition:** AFI Premier Division – Week 3
- **Final Score:** Dublin Rhinos 24 – 12 Galway Gryphons

<!-- excerpt -->

---

### **Game Overview**

Under a grey Dublin sky and a persistent drizzle, the **Dublin Rhinos** secured their second win of the season with a gritty performance against a resilient **Galway Gryphons** side. While the weather made for a slippery pigskin and several fumbled exchanges, the Rhinos’ dominant ground game eventually wore down the visitors' defensive front.

### **Key Moments**

* **First Quarter:** The Rhinos set the tone early. After a 40-yard kick return by Sean O’Neill, the offense marched steadily into the red zone. RB **Cian Murphy** punched it in from 4 yards out to open the scoring. (7-0)
* **Second Quarter:** Galway responded with some creative play-calling. A successful "flea-flicker" caught the Rhinos’ secondary sleeping, leading to a 35-yard touchdown strike. However, a missed PAT left them trailing by one. (7-6)
* **Third Quarter:** The "Emerald Defense" of the Rhinos came alive. Defensive End **Liam Byrne** recorded two sacks in a single drive, forcing a Galway punt that was returned to the 10-yard line. The Rhinos capitalized immediately with a quick slant to the endzone. (17-6)
* **Fourth Quarter:** Galway threatened a comeback with a late rushing TD, but a late-game interception by Rhinos' safety **Eoin Kelly** sealed the result.
