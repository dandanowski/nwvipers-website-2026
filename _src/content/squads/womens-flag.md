The women’s flag team began with just 7 players, most of whom were complete rookies to flag football, in the 2025 season of AFI’s Claddagh Bowl. Through disciplined training in all weather and an emphasis on team spirit and camaraderie, our Viper women made the playoffs in spite of having the smallest (and youngest) roster in the league. That inaugural season has set the tone for future seasons, with sights set on going even further into the post-season in the years to come. If you think you have what it takes to help us accomplish that goal, please [get in touch](/get-in-touch/)!
