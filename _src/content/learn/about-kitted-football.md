Senior-Kitted American Football is one of the most exciting, physical and strategic sports in the world — and here at the Northwest Vipers, we’re proud to bring that energy to the Northwest of Ireland.

This is full-contact American football, played with helmets, shoulder pads and other protective equipment. It’s fast, tactical, hard-hitting, and built on teamwork, discipline and intensity.

## What is Senior-Kitted American Football?
Senior-kitted football is the traditional form of American football, the same style played at the highest level in leagues like the NFL. It combines strength, speed, and strategy, with players competing in specialised positions across offence, defence and special teams.

Within American Football Ireland, senior-kitted games are played over 4 quarters of 12 minutes, with each team battling to move the ball down the field and score touchdowns, field goals, or extra points.

The game is played 11 vs 11, with each team putting eleven players on the field at a time.

This programme is open to players aged 18+, making it perfect for adults looking to try something new, competitive and physically challenging.

Every play is structured, every position matters, and every yard is earned.

## How the Game Works
Each team takes turns playing offence (trying to score) and defence (trying to stop them). The offence has four attempts (downs) to advance at least 10 yards. If successful, they keep possession and continue their drive toward the end zone.

The defence’s job is simple — stop them.

It’s a game of inches, decisions, and execution.

## Why Play Kitted Football?
- Build strength, fitness and resilience
- Learn strategy, discipline and teamwork
- Experience real competition and game-day intensity
- Be part of a growing sport in Ireland

No matter your background — rugby, GAA, soccer, or completely new — there’s a position for you.

We welcome players of all levels, from beginners to experienced athletes. Our coaching staff will guide you through the fundamentals and help you develop into a complete player.
