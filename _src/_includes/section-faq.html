Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Northwest Vipers?

The Northwest Vipers are a non-profit American football club based in Derry, Northern Ireland. We field three teams: a senior kitted team, a women's flag team, and a youth kitted team.

Do I need experience to play?

No! We welcome players of all experience levels. Our coaches provide training and support to help you develop your skills.

How do I join?

Visit our contact page and send us a message. We'll get back to you as soon as possible.

How many teams can I play for?

You can play for as many teams as you are eligible for. For example, if you are a youth player, you can play for the youth team and the senior team.
